OUR STORY

Our company, which started in December 2019, is based in the Philippines and focuses on space restoration and furniture refurbishment by renovating and restoring it to its original state. As the years go by, the company has ventured into mall fit-outs and mall kiosks, considering luxury brands, cosmetics, and restaurants. Given a chance at large-scale planning comes the resorts, rest houses, and dream houses. Disenyo Arka is our trademark name while Arka-Illumina is the name registered on all supporting documents
